LINGUA
FORTUNA

Lingua Fortuna is a non-profit youth organization that promotes cultural and humanitarian cooperation between Belgium and Ukraine.

Our mission? To create social connections, foster collective reflection on today’s and tomorrow’s major challenges, and plant the seeds of hope and positive change.

Welcome, Bienvenue, Ласкаво просимо

Lingua Fortuna was founded in 2018 out of a Belgian-Ukrainian friendship, with the aim of promoting the French language and Belgian culture among Ukrainian youth. It was during this time that the first summer camp for children was organized in Wallonia.

Despite global crises, the pandemic, the full-scale invasion of Ukraine, and other major changes, our organization continues to play an active role in public life while redefining its goals. With the support of the Belgian people, Lingua Fortuna has helped many Ukrainian families and young people evacuate to Belgium and find refuge there. Today, the organization works to strengthen social cohesion among Ukrainians in Belgium and to encourage their civic engagement by creating new public projects and continuing to organize intercultural camps.

Since 2018, we have been organizing intercultural camps. The association aims to foster exchange between peoples by offering young people from Belgium and Ukraine the opportunity to meet in a natural setting, discover other cultures, and discuss topics such as ecology, sexism, and healthy eating.

During the health crisis and pandemic, which limited our freedom of movement, the association continued to work towards its goals by organizing online meetings and participating in various social projects. In collaboration with the World Congress of Ukrainian Youth Organizations, Lingua Fortuna helped raise funds for hospitals in Ukraine, particularly in regions affected by the war.

Unfortunately, in 2022, just as we were planning to fully resume our in-person activities, our program was once again disrupted by the Russian invasion and the terrorist attacks against the Ukrainian people.

Together with the Belgian community, Lingua Fortuna did everything possible to evacuate many young people and their families, offering them temporary protection in Belgium and the opportunity to continue their education.

Since then, Lingua Fortuna has been working to strengthen social cohesion and promote active civic engagement. Its goal is to bring people together and foster integration.

A Few of Our Many Projects

Artivism activities, environmental clean-ups, a volleyball day, the organization of Ukraine’s Independence Day celebration, a seminar in Norway… These are just a few of the projects that keep our non-profit organization vibrant and active.
