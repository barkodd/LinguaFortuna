Welcome, Bienvenue, Ласкаво просимо

Hi, my name is Anton! I created this website.

Hello, my name is Mathilde! I’ve been involved since the very beginning of the non-profit organization Lingua Fortuna, which was born out of a wonderful Belgian-Ukrainian friendship. Over time, the organization’s goals have evolved, reflecting both the geopolitical context and our values and commitments.

Within the nonprofit, I contribute by drawing on my teaching and facilitation skills, which I’ve developed through my work as a French and theater teacher. I provide both educational and personal support, with a desire to build connections, encourage self-expression, and boost everyone’s confidence.

Hello, my name is Oksana. After studying translation and later human rights, and following a career in international settings, I co-founded the non-profit organization Lingua Fortuna, inspired by encounters, intercultural collaboration, and a desire to contribute to a more just and compassionate world.

At LF, I am involved in creating and coordinating educational and community-focused projects, with a particular focus on intercultural dialogue, civic engagement, and well-being. I enjoy creating spaces where everyone can learn, express themselves, build confidence, and turn their ideas into meaningful projects.

Hello, my name is Svitlana. I am a co-founder of the non-profit organization Lingua Fortuna and a trained nurse. I joined LF with a desire to care for others and to contribute, in my own way, to projects that promote humanity and solidarity.

Within the association, I handle aspects related to welcoming, well-being, and ensuring participants feel cared for during our projects. And because caring also involves sharing, my cooking is an integral part of my commitment, creating a true culinary bridge between Belgian, Ukrainian, and other traditions. Through food, I share a little piece of home, I build connections, and I help everyone feel welcomed, safe, and at home.

Hello, my name is Gil. I have been following the Lingua Fortuna adventure since its beginnings and have had the opportunity to participate in two of their projects: one in Belgium – Cine Club – and the other as a Youth Leader during an Erasmus+ exchange in Finland. I fully embrace their vision, which is based on well-being, sharing, learning, and many other human values that I deeply share. As a hockey coach and avid traveler, I strive to pass on my life experience to make their trips and camps even more enriching, both on a human and personal level.

Hello, my name is Florian. I have been working with Lingua Fortuna since early 2026, following my participation as a Youth Leader in an Erasmus+ exchange in Finland. Trained as an actor, I am also a facilitator and theatre teacher. I believe that, in order to foster well-being and meaningful human connections during camps, it is essential from the very beginning to build trust and encourage a sense of community within the group. Drawing on my theatrical and educational experience, this is what I strive to bring.

Hello, my name is Jane!

Above all, I am a mathematics teacher, a profession that has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. Since 2025, I have been part of the Lingua Fortuna organization, which introduced me to the world of Erasmus+ and international youth activities. Through this experience, I discovered new ways of working with young people, and today I also volunteer within the organization.

Hello, my name is William. After studying acting at the Conservatory of Mons (Art²), I embarked on the creation of various projects centered around theatre, as well as cinema. I am also a teacher at an academy: sharing knowledge is a value that is particularly important to me and one that guides my journey.

Within the Lingua Fortuna non-profit organization, I am involved in the co-creation of a filmmaking workshop, with the goal of fostering young people's independence. Together, we write, film, act in, and edit a short film within a collaborative and creative environment.

I am also involved in Erasmus+ projects as a Youth Leader, particularly during international exchanges, such as in Finland in 2025. These intercultural experiences enrich my practice and strengthen my desire to share knowledge through the arts. Through all of this, I try to pass on my love for these creative mediums as best I can.

Hello, my name is Anastasiia! As an administrative assistant at Lingua Fortuna, I help organize events and support the coordination of the association's various activities. I also contribute to writing reports and documenting completed projects, particularly those carried out within the Erasmus+ framework.
